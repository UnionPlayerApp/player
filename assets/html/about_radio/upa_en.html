Broadcasting of the Internet radio station “First Union Radio” began on March 1, 2020. You can listen to us 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

You will learn everything about the life of the Union State of Belarus and Russia. Hear your favorite Belarusian and Russian songs, interviews on current topics and the latest news.

The broadcast includes programs created by order of the Broadcasting Organization of the Union State and aired on the largest radio stations in Russia and Belarus.

Fresh news broadcasts are broadcast every hour on First Union Radio.
